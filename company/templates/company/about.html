About Us

History of Poimen Ministry

God wants all people to hear the good news of Jesus Christ and receive eternal salvation. To hear the gospel, people need to have access to the sound teachings of God’s Word.

We believe that the Church has the primary responsibility for teaching the gospel of Jesus Christ to people. However, the Church needs support to preach and teach the Gospel to believers and reach out to unreached people. Poimen Ministry was established to support the preaching and teaching of the true Gospel of Jesus Christ. Poimen Ministry is a Christian organization registered by the Ethiopian Gospel Believers’ Churches Council and has a legal license to do ministry. Poimen Ministry is governed by seasoned and devoted board of directors.

Vision, Mission and Core Values

Mission

Poimen Ministry exists to provide Bible-based teaching, counseling and holistic support to Christ’s followers to be fruitful in their lives.

Vision

To see believers, Christian families and Church ministers built on the Word of God, live godly life and bring people to Christ and help them grow in Christian faith.

Core Values

The Ministry abides by the following core values

  • Integrity
  • Stewardship
  • Transparency
  • Partnership
